About Esquire

Meet Anthony and Michael, the founders of Esquire Sports! These two have been creating meaningful sports content since 2013, and their resume keeps growing. From Instagram, to YouTube, to TikTok, to Twitter, "Ant and Mike's" journey has been quite the story.

Story

It all started in Arliington Virginia, when Anthony and Michael were both in sixth grade. The two met in P.E., and began discussing their common interests: Sports, Youtube, Sports YouTube... From there, their minds went to work. They founded their first joint channel- a trickshot channel- later that spring. Two years later, they created their own sports instagram pages. They played football together in high school, and were given the joint-nickname of "Esquire" due to their strategic, lawyerly approaches to the sport. When COVID hit in 2020 and the two went off to college, it was Anthony's idea for them both to team up once more, and take on the newest social media frontier... TikTok! The rest is history. Over 120,000 combined followers, over 12 million likes amassed, millions of views... The sports media sphere has some new faces in it now-- Two, to be specific. And it should be no surprise. This has been a long time coming.

TikTok
